Historical Information

Indian Railways, one of the world's largest railway networks, has a rich history dating back to the 19th century. It played a significant role in the development of transportation in India, connecting distant regions and fostering economic growth.

Over the years, Indian Railways has faced numerous challenges related to passenger management, cleanliness, security, and maintenance due to its sheer scale and complexity.
